Bangladesh

Bangladesh is a small and beautiful country in South Asia. We got independence in 1971 from Pakistan after a long war. Because of the sacrifice of millions of freedom fighters we got this country.

Dhaka

The name of the city where I live is Dhaka. It is the capital and largest city of Bangladesh. It is also the 3rd most densely populated city in the world. It lies along the east bank of the Buriganga River.
